About Me

I am a versatile developer driven by the challenge of building complex, high-performance systems. My expertise spans from Full-Stack Web Development and Mobile App Architecture to specialized work in Machine Learning and Deep Learning.

I also have a strong background in Server-Client architecture. I have designed and built fully functioning Server-Client systems from the ground up, with my comprehensive Point of Sale (POS) application standing as concrete proof of my ability to develop robust, distributed data systems.

Beyond standard development, I specialize in Computational Security, having engineered custom Anti-Cheat and Anti-Tamper systems for large-scale interactive environments. Whether I am training neural networks or hardening a system's defenses against exploits, I focus on creating digital experiences that are as secure as they are immersive.
